About Denlux Spaces

30+ Years of Transforming Spaces Across Karnataka with Precision, Quality & Trust

Our Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 2003, Denlux Spaces is led by Ranjitha Bai Balachandra, bringing 30+ years of industry expertise in interior design and construction.

From concept to completion, we transform spaces into functional, elegant environments that reflect your lifestyle and business vision.

We proudly serve all districts of Karnataka, delivering consistent quality, transparent execution, and timeless design.

Our Mission

To transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary living and working environments through innovative design, quality craftsmanship, and client-centric solutions that exceed expectations.

Our Vision

Our Journey
